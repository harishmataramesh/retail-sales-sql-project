## 📊 Business Problems Solved

1. **Top Selling Products**  
   Identify the products with the highest sales volume to support inventory and stock planning.

2. **Customer Segmentation**  
   Categorize customers based on their spending behavior to improve targeted marketing strategies.

3. **Sales Trend Analysis**  
   Analyze monthly or daily sales trends to understand business growth and seasonality.

4. **City-Level Performance**  
   Evaluate revenue generated from different cities to optimize regional business expansion.

5. **Revenue Contribution Analysis**  
   Determine which products or categories contribute the most to overall revenue.
